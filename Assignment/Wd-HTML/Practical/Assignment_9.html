New York City is one of the most iconic cities in the world, known for its bustling energy, towering skyscrapers, and diverse culture. Located in the state of New York, it consists of five boroughs: Manhattan, Brooklyn, Queens, The Bronx, and Staten Island. Famous landmarks such as the Statue of Liberty, Times Square, Central Park, and the Empire State Building attract millions of visitors each year. As a global hub for finance, fashion, art, and entertainment, New York City offers endless opportunities for exploration, with something for everyone, from world-class museums to vibrant neighborhoods.
